Who I Am

Hello! I'm Sandeep — a B.Tech student in Electronics & Communication Engineering at JIIT Noida, maintaining a CGPA of 9.03. I have a genuine passion for the full spectrum of engineering: from designing circuits in Proteus and writing embedded C to building terminal apps and crafting web interfaces.

I believe the best way to learn is to build, break things, and rebuild them better. That philosophy has taken me from blinking LEDs on an Arduino to programming obstacle-avoiding robots, from SQL-powered travel planners to hackathon-born freelancing platforms.

When I step away from the desk, you'll find me on the cricket field — where the same values of patience, teamwork, and reading the situation apply just as much as they do in engineering.
